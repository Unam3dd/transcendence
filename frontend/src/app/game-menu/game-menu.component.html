Rules of the tournament:

Players fight each other in turn, winners of first round will fight against each other next round.

If you are not select for a round and than you don't have been eliminated, it means that there was no opponent for you this round. But you will fight against one of the winner of the actual round on the next round.

If you loose a match, you are eliminated of the tournament.
